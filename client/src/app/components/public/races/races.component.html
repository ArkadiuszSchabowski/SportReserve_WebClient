Race Name: Run for the Animal Shelter
Date: 01.01.2026 (1st January)
Distances: 5K, 10K, 15K
Start Location: Manchester, Heaton Park (near the main entrance off Sheepfoot Lane)
Entry Fee: Free
Start the New Year with a heart! Take part in the Run for the Animal Shelter and make your first step in 2026 a stride towards good. This is a perfect opportunity to combine your New Year's resolutions with invaluable help for animals in need. Join hundreds of running enthusiasts who will collectively traverse the picturesque paths of Heaton Park. What's more, participation in the race is completely free, and the city itself is the organizer, making it even more accessible and supported for everyone who wants to help. During registration, you have the option, but not the obligation, to specify a donation amount, which will be entirely allocated to support local animal shelters. Feel the energy of the community and the satisfaction of running for a noble cause. Become a hero for homeless animals and give your New Year true meaning!
Race Name: Valentine Race with Heart
Date: 14.02.2026 (14th February)
Distances: 5K (run/walk)
Start Locations:
Liverpool: Albert Dock (the square in front of the Royal Liver Building)
Newcastle: Quayside (near the Tyne Bridge)
Brighton: Madeira Drive (near Brighton Palace Pier)
Entry Fee: £10
Celebrate Valentine's Day in a unique way – with an active run from the heart! The Valentine Race with Heart is an ideal proposition for couples, friends, and anyone who wants to spend February 14th in a joyful, sporty atmosphere. Choose one of three charming locations – Liverpool, Newcastle, or Brighton – and feel the magic of Valentine's as you traverse urban landscapes filled with romantic accents. The 5K distance is perfect for both experienced runners and those who prefer a leisurely walk. The entry fee is a symbolic £10, which helps to organize this special event. Every participant also has the option to choose a special Valentine's gadget: a commemorative t-shirt in any size with the possibility of name personalization, a heart-themed mug, or a shaker with a Valentine's accent. This is not just a race; it's a celebration of the love for movement and for loved ones. Sign up today and create unforgettable memories by running together with heart!
Race Name: London Half-Marathon Race
Date: 10.03.2026 (10th March)
Distance: Half-Marathon (21.1K)
Start Location: London, Green Park (near Constitution Hill)
Entry Fee: £25
Dreaming of a race in the heart of one of the world's most iconic capitals? The London Half-Marathon Race is your chance! Prepare for an unforgettable 21.1 kilometers that will lead you through the vibrant streets of London, past majestic landmarks and picturesque parks. Starting from Green Park, you'll run past Buckingham Palace, Big Ben, and the London Eye, admiring views you know from postcards. The entry fee is £25, guaranteeing professional organization and an unforgettable experience. What's more, every participant who finishes the race will receive a commemorative medal with their time engraved. For the top runners, impressive trophies and attractive cash prizes are awarded: £500 for the winner, £250 for second place, and £100 for third place. This is an excellent opportunity to test your endurance and feel the true spirit of London running. Join thousands of participants from around the world who collectively create the unforgettable atmosphere of this prestigious event. Sign up today and become part of London's running history!
