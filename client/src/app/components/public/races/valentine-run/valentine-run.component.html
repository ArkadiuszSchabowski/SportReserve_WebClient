Race Name: Valentine Race with Heart
Date: 14.02.2026 (14th February)
Distances: 5K (run/walk)
Start Locations:
Liverpool: Albert Dock (the square in front of the Royal Liver Building)
Newcastle: Quayside (near the Tyne Bridge)
Brighton: Madeira Drive (near Brighton Palace Pier)
Entry Fee: £10
Celebrate Valentine's Day in a unique way – with an active run from the heart! The Valentine Race with Heart is an ideal proposition for couples, friends, and anyone who wants to spend February 14th in a joyful, sporty atmosphere. Choose one of three charming locations – Liverpool, Newcastle, or Brighton – and feel the magic of Valentine's as you traverse urban landscapes filled with romantic accents. The 5K distance is perfect for both experienced runners and those who prefer a leisurely walk. The entry fee is a symbolic £10, which helps to organize this special event. Every participant also has the option to choose a special Valentine's gadget: a commemorative t-shirt in any size with the possibility of name personalization, a heart-themed mug, or a shaker with a Valentine's accent. This is not just a race; it's a celebration of the love for movement and for loved ones. Sign up today and create unforgettable memories by running together with heart!
