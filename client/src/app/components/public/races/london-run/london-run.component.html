Race Name: London Half-Marathon Race
Date: 10.03.2026 (10th March)
Distance: Half-Marathon (21.1K)
Start Location: London, Green Park (near Constitution Hill)
Entry Fee: £25
Dreaming of a race in the heart of one of the world's most iconic capitals? The London Half-Marathon Race is your chance! Prepare for an unforgettable 21.1 kilometers that will lead you through the vibrant streets of London, past majestic landmarks and picturesque parks. Starting from Green Park, you'll run past Buckingham Palace, Big Ben, and the London Eye, admiring views you know from postcards. The entry fee is £25, guaranteeing professional organization and an unforgettable experience. What's more, every participant who finishes the race will receive a commemorative medal with their time engraved. For the top runners, impressive trophies and attractive cash prizes are awarded: £500 for the winner, £250 for second place, and £100 for third place. This is an excellent opportunity to test your endurance and feel the true spirit of London running. Join thousands of participants from around the world who collectively create the unforgettable atmosphere of this prestigious event. Sign up today and become part of London's running history!
